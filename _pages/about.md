Dr. Feng Cao is currently a senior scientist at Advanced Remanufacturing and Technology Centre (ARTC), A*STAR. Before joining ARTC, he worked as a senior engineer in the field of high speed and high precision motion control in semiconductor industry. He received the Ph.D and the B.S degrees from the School of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering, Nanyang Technological University in 2017 and 2013 respectively. His research interest includes intelligent robot manipulation and motion control.
